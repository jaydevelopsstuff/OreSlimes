Ore Slimes adds 10 new slimes to the game

Pre-Hardmode:
- Copper Slime
- Tin Slime
- Iron Slime
- Lead Slime
- Silver Slime
- Tungsten Slime
- Gold Slime
- Platinum Slime

Hardmode:
- Cobalt Slime
- Palladium Slime

These slimes spawn underground and at varying rates depending on their value. Their health, damage, and defense are also balanced for their value.
